Isomerism: Same molecular formula, different structure.

## 1. Structural Isomers (Connectivity)
- **Chain:** Different carbon skeletons.
- **Position:** Functional group in a different spot.
- **Functional:** Different functional groups (e.g., Alcohols vs. Ethers).

## 2. Stereoisomers (Spatial Arrangement)
- **Geometric (Cis-Trans):** Restricted rotation (Alkenes/Cycloalkanes).
  - **Cis:** Same side.
  - **Trans:** Opposite sides.
- **Optical (Enantiomers):** Chiral center (Carbon with 4 different groups). Non-superimposable mirror images.
